A single pixel art character, 64×64 pixels. PNG. Flat colors with no gradients
or shading. 1px black outline around the full body. Blocky rectangular shapes
for arms and legs — like a GameBoy Color sprite. Chibi proportions: large head
(~50% of height), small body. Standing straight, arms at sides, neutral calm
expression. Eyes are 2×2 black squares with 1 white highlight pixel. Full body
visible from head to feet.

Based on my attached photo — match the skin tone, hair color, hair style, and
clothing. Use maximum 6 colors: 1 skin, 1-2 hair, 1-2 outfit, 1 black outline.

Hard pixel edges everywhere. NO anti-aliasing. NO smooth curves. Every color
boundary is a visible stair-step of pixels. Transparent background. Output
64×64 PNG. Save this — you'll use it as reference for Step 2.

║  STEP 2 — Generate the sprite sheet                 ║

Upload the "character.png" from Step 1 as reference, paste this prompt,

Create a pixel art sprite sheet with 10 rows × 4 columns. Every cell is exactly
64×64 pixels. Total output: 256×640 PNG.

CRITICAL: The character in EVERY cell must match the attached reference image
("character.png") — same face, same colors, same proportions, same style. Only
the POSE changes between cells.

BACKGROUND: Fill all empty space with solid magenta (#FF00FF). No checkerboard.
No gradient. Flat magenta everywhere the character is not present. Do NOT let
magenta bleed into the character's edges.

ROW 1 (IDLE): 4 frames. Gentle breathing bob (2px up/down). Eyes blink between
open and closed. Arms at sides. Calm expression.

ROW 2 (TYPING / WRITING NOTES): 4 frames. Small keyboard strip at bottom (4 gray
keys with black outlines). Keys alternate pressing down — right key, then left
key. Arms raise with finger pressing, body leans side to side. Focused downward
expression. (Reused while the AI is writing notes/MOM in the background.)

ROW 3 (THINKING): 4 frames. Hand under chin. Head tilts left-right. Eyes looking
up. Small blue sweat drop (3-4 blue pixels) on forehead in early frames. One
frame has tiny yellow sparkle near head (idea moment).

ROW 4 (DONE / JUMP): 4 frames. Frame 1: crouch down. Frame 2: spring up, arms
raised in V-shape. Frame 3: peak of jump with sparkle stars near hands. Frame 4:
land with slight bounce, dust pixels at feet. Happy expression throughout.

ROW 5 (DRAGGING / MOCHI): 4 frames. Frame 1: normal standing. Frame 2: body
stretched vertically tall (torso elongated, head stays at same height), stretch
lines on sides. Frame 3: body squished horizontally wide, puffed cheeks. Frame 4:
springing back to normal, dizzy eyes.

ROW 6 (POUNCE / HUNT): 4 frames. Frame 1: crouched low, arms pulled back,
narrowed eyes. Frame 2: launching forward, arms reaching ahead, speed lines
trailing. Frame 3: mid-air flight, body stretched. Frame 4: landing with impact
dust, satisfied smirk.

ROW 7 (HAPPY / PETTED): 4 frames. ^_^ happy squinting eyes. Pink blush squares
on cheeks. Tiny pixel hearts (pink, black outline) floating near head — growing
larger and multiplying across frames. Sparkle stars in later frames. Content
expression.

ROW 8 (ALERT): 4 frames. Surprised expression — eyes wide open (full white
squares with small black pupils, taller than normal). Mouth open in a small "O".
Frame 1: standing upright but startled. Frame 2: body shaking left. Frame 3:
body shaking right. Frame 4: standing upright again. A small yellow exclamation
mark "!" above the head in frames 2 and 3. Quick urgent vibe — meeting/event
about to start.

ROW 9 (RECORDING): 4 frames. Character wearing big over-ear pixel headphones
(gray cups with a black headband connecting them). Standing calmly, gentle bob
(2px) like idle but slower. Eyes half-closed in concentration. Frames cycle
through tiny mouth movements as if quietly listening. NO red dot in the sprite
itself — the app draws a separate pulsing red recording indicator above the
character at runtime. Just give us the focused, headphones-on listening pose.

ROW 10 (MOM READY / NOTES DONE): 4 frames. Character proudly holding up a
small folded paper / notebook with a checkmark "✓" on it. Frame 1: paper just
appeared, character looking at it. Frame 2: paper raised higher, broad smile,
sparkle stars near the paper. Frame 3: paper at peak height, character winking
or doing a thumbs-up with the free hand, more sparkles. Frame 4: paper lowered
slightly, content satisfied smile. Yellow/gold sparkles throughout — the
"task complete" moment.

STYLE (same as reference): Flat colors, no gradients. 1px black outline around
character. Hard pixel edges with NO anti-aliasing. Blocky rectangular limbs.
Same color palette as the reference image in every cell. Full body always
visible. Magenta background (#FF00FF) — the magenta IS the background, not
part of the art.

OUTPUT: 256×640 PNG. 10 rows × 4 columns. Magenta background everywhere.
